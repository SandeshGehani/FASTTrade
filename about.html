About FASTTrade

Born from the vibrant halls of FAST-NUCES, FASTTrade is more than just a marketplace - it's a community-driven platform created for the entire FAST family. We understand the unique needs of both students and faculty members, from managing tight student budgets to finding affordable teaching resources and research equipment.

Our mission is to foster a culture of sharing and collaboration within the FAST community. Students can buy, sell, and trade textbooks, tech gadgets, and other essentials with their peers. Faculty members can exchange teaching materials, research equipment, and academic resources. Whether you're a student looking for affordable course materials or a professor seeking specialized equipment, FASTTrade connects you with trusted members of your university community.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a listing?

To create a listing, click on the "Create Listing" button in the navigation bar. Fill in the required details about your item, add photos, and set your price. Once submitted, your listing will be reviewed and published.

Is FASTTrade only for FAST-NUCES students?

Yes, FASTTrade is exclusively for FAST-NUCES students. You need a valid FAST email address to register and use the platform.

How do I contact a seller?

You can contact a seller by clicking on the "Message" button on their listing. This will open a chat where you can discuss the item and arrange the details of the transaction.
